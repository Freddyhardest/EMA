This form allows the user to update their membership details with the O.U. Walking Club. The user can enter their preferred payment method, date, email, phone number and alternative contact number. The user can also indicate whether they consent to receive announcement emails by entering Y or N in the appropriate field. The data entered by the user can be submitted by clicking a "Submit" button.
